Spy x Family is an ongoing anime series based on the manga of the same name by Tatsuya Endo.
The series follows a three people pretending to be a family even as they protect their own bizarre secrets. The father,
Loid, is a secret agent; The mother, Yor, is an assassin; And the daughter, Anya, is a telepath.
The series follows the “Forgers” as they each juggle maintaining their secret identities and jobs with home life.
